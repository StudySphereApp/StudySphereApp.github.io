# 🌐 Study Sphere

**Study Sphere** is a modern, beautifully designed Android productivity application tailored for students and professionals. Built to boost focus and organize study materials, the app combines customizable focus timers, task tracking, and a personal knowledge library into one seamless, visually stunning experience.

## ✨ Key Features

- **⏱️ Focus Hub (Pomodoro, Clock, Stopwatch)**: Minimize distractions with a dedicated focus screen. Features a fully customizable Pomodoro timer—simply tap the time to adjust your focus duration on the fly.
- **🎨 Immersive Personalization**: Create your ideal study environment. Upload custom image wallpapers or ambient video loops to use as your focus screen background, complete with a video mute toggle. You can even import your own `.ttf` and `.otf` fonts to personalize the aesthetic of the timers and clocks.
- **📁 Library & Notes Organization**: A clean, file-manager style dashboard to organize your notes, subjects, and study materials into customizable folders. 
- **✅ Task Management**: Integrated To-Do list functionality to easily keep track of assignments, deadlines, and daily objectives.
- **💎 Modern Jetpack Compose UI**: Built with Material Design 3 guidelines, offering intuitive interactions, edge-to-edge layouts, and an elegant glassmorphic aesthetic.
- **🔒 Offline-First & Private**: Your study materials, notes, timers, and custom wallpapers are all persisted locally on your device for absolute privacy and zero-latency performance.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Language**: Kotlin
- **UI Toolkit**: Jetpack Compose (Material 3)
- **Architecture**: MVVM (Model-View-ViewModel)
- **Local Persistence**: Room Database for notes & files, SharedPreferences for user settings
- **Media**: Coil (Image loading), Native VideoView (Video wallpapers)
- **Concurrency**: Kotlin Coroutines & Flow
