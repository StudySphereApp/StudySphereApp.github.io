Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a ChatGPT API key?

No! You do not need to connect an API. You simply use your own AI account (like ChatGPT or Gemini) to generate the text, and then paste it directly into our app.

Can I edit the notes after they are generated?

Yes, the app features a full markdown editor allowing you to tweak, add, or remove information from the generated UI blocks before saving.

Is it available offline?

Yes, your saved library and drafts are stored securely on your device, meaning you can revise your generated notes even without an internet connection.

Are the exported PDFs watermarked?

Currently, exported PDFs are completely clean and ready for professional academic use or sharing with your peer groups.
